Our Facilities

Our facilities consist of the tea room and craft shop.
We also host a general village store, post office and bed and breakfast facilities.
The tea room is of the highest quality, serving many quality teas sourced from around the world, right to your cup.

Our tea room consists of a massive range of loose leaf tea, with over sixty types available, as well as other hot and cold drinks.
Our village store offers all kinds of amenities, from snacks to more useful items while on your trip.

The post office contains an assortment of locally stylised holiday cards, as well as general postal needs such as envelopes and stamps.
